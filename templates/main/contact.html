Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline and milestones during our initial consultation to ensure clear expectations.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. This includes regular updates, security patches, technical support, and feature enhancements as your business grows.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Django, Flutter, and various databases. We choose the best technology stack based on your specific project requirements, scalability needs, and long-term goals.

How do you handle project communication and updates?

We maintain transparent communication through regular email updates, video calls, and project management tools. You'll receive weekly progress reports and have direct access to our team throughout the project. We believe in keeping you informed every step of the way.

What's your pricing structure?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on the scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. We offer flexible payment terms and ensure no hidden costs - everything is transparent from the start.
